About Me

I'm Cait Sidener and I am a full-stack web developer. I graduated from the University of Washburn with my BA in French, and earned my teaching licensure with an additional endorsement in English. For five years, I taught both languages in the public schools of Kansas (and one year in Virginia). Learning language has always been my passion, and helping others learn how to communicate is one of my strengths.

In my former life as an educator, I learned how to effectively communicate with colleagues to build a curriculum from scratch. These days, I put those skills to use collaborating with other web developer to build clean code. I made this major career switch, because I am passionate about my work as a full-stack developer. I am always challenged to discover something new, and truly love using my skills to solve a problem.
